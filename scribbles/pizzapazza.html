pizzapazza

game where you make crazy pizzas with increasingly absurd ingredients

pizzas will be made from scratch and will be placed in the oven, and finally receive a rating

ingredients can include: tomato sauce, cheese, vegetables, meat, fish, fruit, flowers, other pizzas, other food types like cakes, cookies, beverages, bottles empty or full, cutlery, cash, mobile phones, small pets and so on and so forth

rating is based on many criteria such as cooked amount (raw, overcooked, just right), flavour profile, ingredient quality, visuals (color, texture etc), originality and many other criteria to the point of making it impossible to maximise them all

the game interface is the workspace of the pizzaiolo, and the game is played by the first pov, and you can see flour, water, lievito, boxes of ingredients, oven, plates, other random stuff

game must be silly and quick and messy and full of sound effects
