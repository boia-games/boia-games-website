billionaimer

a game where you move a hand pointing the index finger 👉 around on the screen and you press space to launch a TAX forward

from the right side appear at random intervals the pixelated but recognisable faces of billionaires

the player launches taxes on them and when hit they drop sacks of money 💰 which can be picked up by moving on them

if the player is touched by a billionaire, they lose a life

after a while the wave is over, and the player can invest the money they picked up in various categories:

❤️ healthcare = +1 player life

🎓 education = tax is bigger and will earn more money

🏘️ housing = adds a house which blocks the path of billionaires

👥 union = adds a finger nearby, shooting one additional tax

each wave spawns billionaires faster and more frequently. when the player's lives reach 0, it's game over
